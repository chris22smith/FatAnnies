FATANNIE'S

Welcome to Fat Annie's, established in 2014 after an inspiring trip to New York. We started out from a cart on the streets of Manchester's iconic Northern Quarter selling quality hot dogs, as well as feeding festival goers and football fans.

In 2017 we got our first permanent home in Leeds Kirkgate Market in the Food Hall.

In late 2020 we went totally meat free and expanded our menu to include a range of burgers and banging sides.
